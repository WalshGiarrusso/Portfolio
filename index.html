My name is Walsh Giarrusso.

I’m currently a senior in high school, as well as a self-taught programmer, graphic designer, and music producer. I also use my time to advocate for things I think are important, like web accessibility and police reform. I look up to Robert F. Kennedy, John Lewis, Angela Merkel, and Muhammad Yunus. I believe we all have a duty to try to leave the world a better place than we found it. This website serves as a hub for my work, including both my art and my advocacy.
