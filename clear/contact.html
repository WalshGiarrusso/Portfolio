My name is Walsh Giarrusso.

I’m currently a senior in high school, as well as a self-taught programmer, graphic designer, and music producer. I want to enable others to succeed and be there for people who wouldn't otherwise have someone in their corner. I also use my time to advocate for things I think are important, like web accessibility and police reform. I look up to Tammy Duckworth, Muhammad Yunus, John Lewis, and Robert F. Kennedy. I believe we all have a duty to try to leave the world a better place than we found it. This website serves as a hub for all of my work, including my service, my art, and my advocacy.
