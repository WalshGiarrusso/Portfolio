Gloriosa - Music, November 17th, 2020

I made this after a long period away from music. I'm extremely happy with it. It may be my favorite song that I've ever made. I think that it strikes the balance between repetition and variation, and I love the sound selection. There is a lot of variation in timbre in this song that's unlike anything I've ever made before. The quality of my mixes are pretty consistent, and this is definitely on the higher end of that spectrum, but the areas in which this song really shines are sound selection and composition.
