Nurtured Accretion - Digital, September 3rd, 2020

Open image in a new tab >

This image was created in order to show progress since the last time I had tried to more actively use digital photo manipulation for artistic purposes, with Outlook. I think it shows how far I've come pretty well. If I were to change anything, I would make the background removal on the Mars planet cleaner; currently it's distractingly messy. Overall, I'm extremely happy with Nurtured Accretion and happy to continue my improvement in digital art.
